About Dalia Katan

As a first-generation American and innovation strategist, Dalia is championing conversation on how diversity and human connection can unlock untapped growth for organizations. Amongst her advisory work with startups and F500 companies, Dalia founded the Refugee Workplace Integration Program at Deloitte, celebrating inclusion as the single most powerful tool for growth. She is committed to empowering organizations to successfully utilize and nurture the talents of minorities and her work at the intersection of inclusion, the future of work, and business growth has been published at Deloitte and Princeton University.
