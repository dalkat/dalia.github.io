Challah Bake

Kitchen Items
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon (bulk here)
  • Baking tray
  • Kitchen towel
  • Cooking brush (like this)



Ingredients
  • 1 packet of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1.5 tsp. salt
  • 4-6 cups of flour (you will start with 4)
