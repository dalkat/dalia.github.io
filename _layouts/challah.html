Challah Bake

Servings
Makes 2 medium challahs, or one large challah

Kitchen Items
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon (bulk here)
  • Foil-lined Baking tray
  • Kitchen towel
  • Pastry brush (like this)
  • Optional: disposal gloves to keep hands dough-free

Ingredients
  • 1 packet of Active Dry Yeast (this kind)
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 egg yolk (for coating/egg wash)
  • 1.5 tsp. salt
  • 4-6 cups of all-purpose flour (you will start with 4)
  • Optional: 1.5 cups chocolate chips or raisins
  • Optional: sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or sprinkles
Many thanks to Chabad SF for inspiring the recipe!
Instructions
  1. Combine yeast, sugar, and warm water in mixing bowl. Mix and allow to stand for 5-10 minutes (yeast should produce some bubbles in the water).
  2. Beat the egg in a small cup, then mix in egg, oil, and salt with rest of mixture.
  3. Gradually add the flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition to avoid clumps of flower. As mixture thickens, begin kneading the dough. Continue adding flour as needed and knead dough for 5-10 minutes until dough is smooth and soft, and no longer too sticky.
  4. Optional: If adding chocolate chips or raisins, work into dough as you add the flour in the previous step.
  5. Shape the dough into a ball and leave in the mixing bowl. Cover bowl with slightly damp kitchen towel and let the dough rise for an hour (if you have time: let it rise for 1-2 hours, punch it down to remove air, then let it rise again for another hour).
  6. Once the dough has risen, split the dough into three equal parts (six if you're making two challahs) and roll into long logs on a lightly floured surface or cutting board. Then braid the challah.
  7. Line a baking tray with foil and lightly flour or oil the surface. Set the braided challah dough on the tray and set it aside for 30-60 minutes to continue rising (option to cover with towel).
  8. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Brush the challah with egg yolk (could mix with a bit of water to loosen up the egg yolk). Optionally sprinkle with sesame/poppy seeds or sprinkles! Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Enjoy!
